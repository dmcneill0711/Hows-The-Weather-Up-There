Getting Blown Away:

Easy, Breezy, Beautiful Wind Speeds

When plotting the recorded wind speed/ windiness of a city on the same graph as the latitude of the recorded city, we can see that there are no discernible trends, unless that trend is being indiscernible. Therefore, latitude is NOT a key factor when it comes to predicting the windiness of a city. The graph shows that there is little to no correlation and that there must be something else that is affecting this.
